Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expenses and generate spending summaries.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or error monitoring. These providers are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify. We do not integrate with advertising networks or social media trackers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the app or at our support email. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 7 business days.
